Qing Dynasty

The Last Imperial Dynasty of China

Timeline of Major Events of the Qing Dynasty:

  • 1636 - The Qing Dynasty was founded.
  • 1644 - Prince Dorgon defeated Li Zhicheng put Emperor Shunzi on the throne, which marked the beginning of the rule of the Qing Dynasty in China.
  • 1611 - Kangxi became Emperor.
  • 1722 - Yongzheng became Emperor.
  • 1736 - Qianlong became Emperor.
  • 1793 - McCartney Embassy, led by George McCartney, the first British diplomatic mission to China.
  • 1839 - Opium War, also known as the first Trade War.
  • 1842 - The Treaty of Nanking was signed between Britain and China.
  • 1850 - Tai Ping Rebellion
  • 1861 - Empress Dowager Cixi ruled.
  • 1894 - Sino-Japanese War.
  • 1895 - The Treaty of Shimonoseki was signed between Japan and China.
  • 1900 - The Boxer Rebellion
  • 1905 - The Imperial Examination was abolished.
  • 1908 - Cixi Died.
  • 1911 - Wuchang Uprising.
  • 1912 - Emperor Puyi was abdicated, which marked the end of Qing Dynasty.
